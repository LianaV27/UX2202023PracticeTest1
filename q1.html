Emphasis and strengthened text

Using the tags for strong and emphasis is a better choice than using the italics and bold tags because the former tags signal the importance and describe the effect of the affected text better than the latter ones through the name of the tags. Bolding a word or phrase creates a strong effect. It gives the word both figurative and literal weight, drawing attention to it and implying importance. Using italics creates emphasis, telling readers that this word or phrase is important, while often indicating a change in tone and the connotation of the word or phrase.
